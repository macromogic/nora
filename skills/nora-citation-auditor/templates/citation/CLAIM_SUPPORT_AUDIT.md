# Claim Support Audit

This file is a human-in-the-loop reading aid, not an automated verification record. A `SUPPORTED` entry means the cited source's available text appeared consistent with the claim to the agent that reviewed it — it is not a guarantee of scientific correctness. Treat every entry as a starting point for human review, not a conclusion.

Append-only. Do not delete or edit past entries when adding new ones — add a new entry, or add a `resolution` note to an existing one.

## Legend

- `SUPPORTED`: source directly and clearly supports the claim as stated, including its strength.
- `PARTIALLY_SUPPORTED`: source supports part of the claim, or under narrower conditions than stated.
- `WEAK_OR_INDIRECT`: source is topically related but doesn't clearly establish the claim.
- `UNSUPPORTED`: source does not support the claim, or appears to contradict it.
- `REVIEW_REQUIRED`: partial information; a human with the full text should make the call.
- `BLOCKED`: not enough material was available to say anything useful.
